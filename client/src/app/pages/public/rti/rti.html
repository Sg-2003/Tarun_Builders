Transparency

Right to Information Act, 2005

In accordance with Section 4(1)(b) of the RTI Act, we maintain full transparency of our public operations, officer contact channels, and corporate policies.

RTI Statutory Declaration

Tarun Builders, being a Class-A Government Registered Contractor partnering on public-sector infrastructure contracts, is committed to transparency. We voluntarily declare contact channels for public audit, Grievance redressal, and information routing in compliance with standard government directives.

RTI Applications Submission

Citizens can submit applications under the RTI Act, 2005. A fee of Rs. 10/- (or as prescribed by rules) should be paid in the form of a Demand Draft / Postal Order drawn in favor of "Tarun Builders", payable at Mumbai, or online via public gateway transfers.

Company Profile

Shaping the Nation's Landscape Since 2005

Tarun Builders is a premier ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015 certified infrastructure development company. As a Class-A Government registered contractor, we have been delivering state-of-the-art civil construction, highways, metro systems, and smart city infrastructure.

Over the past two decades, we have established ourselves as a benchmark of excellence, reliability, and integrity. We collaborate closely with major public sector enterprises, municipal corporations, and national authorities to deliver complex projects on time and within budget.
